The Best Movies According to Taylor

My Top 3 Movies of All-Time

The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

The first film in the greatest adventure to ever have been written by the father of fantasy.
No other movie gives me such a feeling of warmth and peace.

The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

The sequel to my favorite movie, though still just a part of one large story, which was
one large book. The Fellowship is broken, but the journey continues.

The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The epic conclusion to the greatest story ever told. Who you thought was the protagonist
succumbs to darkness in the end, but the true hero reveals himself. The King reclaims his
throne and the enemy is defeated.
